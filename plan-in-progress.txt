  What landed

  The keystone you steered me to ‚ÄĒ in-out (read-write) buffers. A custom

  func‚ÜíEff run-site: bind an in-out out-param to alias its in-arg buffer (it
  was binding null). So net.recv(conn, buf)(filled:, r:); data := filled[0:r]
  works ‚ÄĒ no move, copy, or echo.

  seq composes func-built Effs. (eff_nested_builder, eff_seq_funcs,
  eff_nested_length)
  - Empty Eff() from a void extern, bare () run. (eff_void)
  - |> reshaping ‚ÄĒ type-changing finalizers (syscall(2,‚Ä¶) |> to_fd, i64‚Üífd) now
  retype the Eff (tycheck + codegen).
  - In-out out-param wiring. (eff_inout_buffer)
  - syscall pointer-arg coercion extended to move-wrapped names, []char consts,
  and slice exprs (CharBuf.cb[0:cap]).
  - Earlier: purity-sees-Eff-run, purity-param-shadow, Eff-run binding
  precedence.
